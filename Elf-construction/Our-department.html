Executive/Leadership department

The department is led by an experienced executive leadership team who provide strategic direction and oversight. They work closely with contractors who carry out key projects, investors who provide capital to support growth, and a team of skilled lawyers who offer legal guidance around contracts, compliance and risk management. The diverse expertise of the executives, contractors, investors and lawyers enables the department to operate effectively.

consultancy/design department

The department is home to a team of architects, designers, 3D model creators, a surveyor and an experienced supervisor. The architects and designers come up with creative and functional plans, the 3D modelers bring the designs to life digitally, the surveyor analyzes sites and provides layouts, and the supervisor oversees the collaboration to turn the visions into reality. This assembled group works together to deliver exciting new building designs.

Electrical and sanitary installation department

The department employs electricians to handle electrical systems, plumbers to install pipes and plumbing fixtures, security installation technicians to set up access systems, cameras and alarms, and a designer who provides plans and aesthetics for the technical components. This capable team coordinates to outfit buildings with essential utilities and safety features.

structural and construction site management

The department contains a structural engineer who oversees the integrity of the building framework, a site engineer who manages on-site development, a supervisor who coordinates the team, a designer who creates plans and aesthetics, and a foreman who directs the on-site construction crew. This knowledgeable group works cohesively to take building plans from concept to completion.

Finance department

The department employs accountants to handle finances and billing, a quantity surveyor to estimate material and labor costs, and a storekeeper to manage inventory and equipment. This team oversees the budgeting, costs and supplies to keep projects on track financially.

project schedule and labour department

The department contains a project manager who oversees the overall construction plans, a supervisor who coordinates the on-site workers, a timekeeper who tracks worker hours and productivity, and a foreman who directs the daily construction crew activities. This organized team works to ensure efficient project execution from start to finish.

Technical department

The department houses technology roles including a software engineer who develops customized applications and programs, a system developer who implements and integrates software platforms, and a technology officer who oversees emerging tech for the company. This tech-focused team handles in-house IT needs to equip the construction business with digital solutions.

Advertisment and social media management

this department contains a social media manager who oversees the brand's online presence, content creator who develops engaging text/image posts, a camera crew that captures high-quality photos/videos of projects, and editors who polish content before publication. This creative team handles marketing and promotions to showcase the construction company's work.

Market controlling department

This department employs commercial marketers to promote services, tender experts to pursue bids and proposals, cost investigators to provide accurate project pricing, and brokers to establish beneficial partnerships. This skilled team drives sales and secures profitable construction projects.

Field operations department

This department contains drivers who transport equipment and materials to job sites, as well as machinery operators who expertly maneuver excavators, bulldozers, cranes and other heavy construction equipment needed to complete projects. This essential team oversees on-site logistics and earthmoving work.

operation support department

This department contains a secretary who manages scheduling and communications, a reporter who tracks project progress, a supervisor who oversees department operations, and executive liaisons who coordinate directly with company leadership. This team provides vital organizational support and ensures alignment between the department and executives.

temporary legal department

The temporary legal department consists a team of contracted lawyers who provide advisory services for a specific construction project. This experienced group handles reviewing contracts, mitigating project risks, resolving disputes, and ensuring regulatory compliance.The legal team provides crucial counsel to avoid legal issues and liabilities during the construction phase of the project. Once the project is complete, the temporary department will dissolve.
